Jeans are one of the most searched-for fashion items worldwide, with an average of 13 searches per second.

To compile this data deep dive into denim, global fashion search platform Lyst has tracked more than 60 million denim related queries over the last 12 months, crunching the queries, page views and sales data across 5 million fashion products from 12,000 designers and stores.

Searches for all things denim have increased 11% year on year, with shoppers looking for hundreds of different combinations of styles, colours, shapes and fits.
